# Account Deletion Request

## Delete your account in the app

The easiest way to delete your Personal Trainer account and all associated data is directly within the app:

1. Open Personal Trainer
2. Go to **Menu** (bottom navigation)
3. Scroll to **Account** section
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm deletion

Your account and all associated data (profile, workout plans, session history) will be permanently deleted immediately.

## Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing:

**homutskih@gmail.com**

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

## What data is deleted

- Your account credentials
- Personal profile (name, age, weight, height, fitness goals)
- Workout plans
- Session history and progress data

All data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
