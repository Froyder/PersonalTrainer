# Privacy Policy for Personal Trainer

Last updated: May 2026

## Data We Collect
- Email address (for authentication via Firebase)
- Fitness profile data (age, weight, height, goals)
- Workout history and session logs

## How We Use Your Data
- To generate personalized workout plans via Google Gemini AI
- To sync your data across devices via Firebase Firestore
- To send workout reminders (only if you enable notifications)

## Third Party Services
- **Firebase** (Google) — authentication and data storage
- **Google Gemini** — AI workout plan generation

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
